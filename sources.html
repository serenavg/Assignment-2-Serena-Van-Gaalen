Biography

I was born and raised in Lethbridge, Alberta. Some of my favourite things to do around the Southern Alberta area are skiing, hiking, camping, and fishing. I played soccer for about 10 years and I 've been a referee for almost 4 years now.

I went to Briercrest college for a semester (fall 2021), but I realized it wasn't the place I wanted to be. So I took some time off from school, worked, and took time to relax and learn about myself. In about May 2022 I was looking at what to do in the fall. The New Media program caught my interest because I saw a class that was on memes. I wanted to do art and I figured this program is the most likely out of any art degree to get me a job after.

First semester at university has been fun but challenging. There are a lot of projects assigned, a lot of papers to read and write, and not enough time in the day. I've made some great friends and already have amazing memories though. Because I have met so many great people, univeristy is a lot of fun.

University of Lethbridge

It has been a good place for me so far. I'm learning a lot in my classes, and I'm learning even more about myself and the world.

The U of L has always been on my radar as a post-secondary school to attend, considering I have lived here in Lethbridge my whole life. As of right now I'm pretty sure I made the right decision in coming here. There is a lot to do and explore around campus
